About CostBrain

After being given a small grant to research the restaurant industry, we conducted a study across 50 restaurants. The result of our study concluded, food cost management is a problem facing most restaurants.

We created CostBrain as a restaurant solution, to make everything from recipe costing to invoice entry a much easier process.
